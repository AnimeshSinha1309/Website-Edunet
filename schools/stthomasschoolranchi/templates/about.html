St. Thomas

Welcome to St. Thomas School, Ranchi, one of the premier educational institutions in the capital city of Jharkhand. With a sole motive of all-round development, this school has been involved in the Formation of Good, Effective & Creative Citizens for more then 36 years. From Troy to California, from London to Calcutta, from New Jersy to Bombay, you can find an X-Thomasian everywhere on this earth.
